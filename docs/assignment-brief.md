## Assignment 2b — Team Presentation

**Type:** Team-based, individually assessed · **Weighting:** 20% · **Due:** Week 13, in class

### Task

An oral presentation, part of the proposal project. Present and respond to audience
questions.

> The presentation content should be based on your report assignment, but **it should not
> simply be an explanation of the report**. Rather, view your presentation as an opportunity
> to **pitch the proposal to the same specific stakeholders** for whom you are preparing the
> report. You need to **persuade** that audience of the merits of your proposed solution.
> You should emphasize the **clear benefits of your proposal for their needs**.

The stakeholder audience is **IMDA** — a statutory board, not a commercial operator. Frame
value as officer workload, turnaround reliability, and preserved regulatory discretion, not
profit.

### Constraints

- **No more than 20 minutes.** "The tutor will ensure the time limit is strictly adhered to."
- **Q&A session follows** each group's presentation. **Every member must participate** in Q&A.
- **Equal airtime** is expected across all five members.
- Slides must help the audience follow with ease.
- **IEEE citation style** for all source material; **a reference list on the final slide**.
- Slides are submitted as part of the assessment.

### Learning outcomes assessed

- Share key, relevant information in a logical manner to engage the audience
- Use language accurately (spoken grammar, pronunciation)
- Use language appropriately to achieve various functions (describe, evaluate, infer)
- Apply non-verbal skills to complement delivery
- Design visual aids to communicate key ideas
- Perform effectively as a cohesive team
